Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

MailGuard helps you scan Gmail messages for potential risks. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Process

  • Your email address, message identifiers, and metadata needed to fetch and analyze emails.
  • Indicators about links and attachments (counts, types, and domains) for risk analysis.

How We Use Data

  • To provide the scanning service and show results in the app.
  • To improve detection quality and service reliability.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with trusted service providers only to operate the system.

Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can disconnect at any time to revoke access.

Your Choices

You can request access or deletion of data by contacting us.
